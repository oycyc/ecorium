Transportation

The transportation sector is the largest contributor of U.S. greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions, accounting for 29% of total U.S. GHG emissions. The United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has launched various programs to reduce GHG emissions from the transportation sector, including the Renewable Fuel Standard Program and the SmartWay light duty program.
